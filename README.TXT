---SOKOBAN---
A puzzle game of pushing rocks onto switches

---HOW TO PLAY---
Move your guy around using the arrow keys.
You can push rocks (black circles). But you can't pull them!
To win, push all the rocks onto their homes (hollow circles).

---CONTROLS---
Arrow keys - move

'[' or ']' - warp to previous/next level
             (can't go past levels you haven't beaten)

'R'        - reset current level
             (use this if you make a mistake causing the level to become unwinnable)

'U' or     - undo last move
BACKSPACE    (up to 15 moves will be remembered)

'Q' or     - quit game
ESCAPE       (your current level and highest level beaten will be saved for next time)
